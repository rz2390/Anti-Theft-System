Anti-Theft System for Home Protection

A super safe home entrance mointering system to protect a house.

We developed a mointering system at the entrance of a house by distinguishing different family members and send message alert to family members when detecting any possible unexpected incomer of home. We primarily used microprocessors including a Raspberry Pi 3 board as well as an Arduino board. The sensors we used are Piezo Vibration sensor and Ultrasonic sensor. This monitoring system has accuracy of detecting stranger up to nearly 100% after proper training of models consisting of all the family members.

Motivation

Motivation

Smart Home Concept

One of the hottest topic in the world of Internet of Things (IOT) is the concept of "Smart Home". Imagine how exciting it could be if you do have a smart home equipped with IOT technology - You may remotely control every piece of domestic applicance via Internet. And even cooler, your electronics could function normally with you absence without consuming too much electricity - you could equip your home with a variety of sensors so as to "monitor" your home environment and consuming very little amount of power and start themselves when detecting corresponding environment changes.

Particularly, in this project, the product we constructed is a system composed of a bounch of sensors and microprocessors which can monitor the entrance of home to detect any possible stranger incomer.

System

Architecture

Particulary, in this project, we aim to develop a simple foot step detection system for entrance safety - for example, home protection. The vibration sensor from the embedded system is supposed to detect the identity of the person entering the entrance. Also, if a stranger whose footstep type is distinguishably different from the home member, the sensor should message the user with any possible unexpected entrance.

Technical Components

Microprocessors and Sensors

Ultrasonic Sensors

1. Microprocessor: We chose Raspberry Pi 3 board as our microprocessor because it has graphic interface which is conveient for us to train models. Also, it has more powerful processing ability than usual popular microprocessors such as Arduino or Huzzah. Moreover, the OS in SD card enable us to process the data locally in an easier way. However, compared to Arduino, it cannot read analog signal directly from sensor, for which reason we implement an ADC module for data collection from sensor.
2. Sensors: Piezo Vibration sensor is used to detect the footstep pattern which record both the tensity and pace of footsteps. Ultrasonic sensors are used to detect the firgure of the incomer and are placed with interval in between to avoid interference.

Prototype

Overall View of System

As can be seen above, the whole system is built to mimic the entrance door of the house. The wood board is the pathway through which any incomer should walk through after he/she enters the door of house. The ultrasonic group is attached into the door frame to detect the incomer's figure. We can say that both of these two "set of sensors" are functioning for detecting the stranger. Only the incomers who can pass both of these two steps of "identity checking" can be detected as a family member, otherwise, message and email alert will be sent out instantly.

Results

Basically, everytime when someone enters the home, an email will be automatically sent to the family member telling them which member it is. The result is virtually a visualized version of the results of our non-linear SVM learning program - It provides you with both the results (which member came in) and the score calculated by the algoritm for each family member's trained model.

However, whe there is a stranger came in, the family member will not only receive an email saying "Alert, Stranger might have come in", but also get a message notification which should catch the user attention more quickly by user hopefully.
